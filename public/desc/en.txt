This mod gives a WoT PC-like capture progress bar.
- The vertical area is shrinked
- The capture progress is shown always (Supremacy, Encounter when there are players from different teams on the base)
- Only one file modified: no HUDLayer.yaml modifications needed. Can be useful for modpacks

Compatibility:
- Platform: any
- Game publisher: any
- Game version: any
- Game version tested against: 9.7.0
